This prompt compares two or more concepts by identifying similarities and differences.

## 2. Experiential Perspective Prompt

### Definition

This prompt asks AI to answer from a specific person's perspective.

---

## Prompt

“As a manufacturing engineer, explain the benefits of using IoT and AI in industrial automation.”

---

## AI Response

“Using AI and IoT in manufacturing helps monitor machines in real time, predict failures before breakdowns occur, reduce downtime, and improve production efficiency. It also supports remote monitoring and energy optimization.”

---

### Observation

The response was realistic and context-aware.

---

## 3. Everyday Functioning Prompt

### Definition

This prompt explains how technology is used in daily operations.

---

## Prompt

“Describe how AI-based predictive maintenance works in everyday manufacturing operations.”

---

## AI Response

“AI systems continuously collect machine data using sensors. The system analyzes vibration, temperature, and performance patterns to predict failures before they occur, helping factories avoid unexpected downtime.”

---

## Observation

The explanation was simple and practical.

---

## 4. Universal Prompt Structures

### Definition

General-purpose prompt templates that can be reused for multiple topics.

---

## Prompt 1

“Explain industrial automation in simple terms with an example.”

### AI Response

“Industrial automation uses machines and computer systems to perform manufacturing tasks automatically. For example, robotic arms in car factories assemble vehicle parts without human intervention.”

---

## Prompt 2

“Explain the process of predictive maintenance step by step.”

### AI Response

1. Sensors collect machine data
2. AI analyzes machine behavior
3. Abnormal patterns are detected
4. Maintenance alerts are generated
5. Repairs are scheduled before failure

---

## Observation

The structure improved clarity and learning effectiveness.

## 5. Prompt Size Limitations

### Definition

Large prompts are divided into smaller manageable parts to improve AI processing.

---

## Example Prompt

### Refined Prompt:

“Explain 10 applications of AI in manufacturing at a time.”
